Our Story

Crafted with passion, served with love

Founded in 2018, Aroma started as a small dream to bring specialty coffee to our neighborhood. Every bean is carefully sourced from sustainable farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, then roasted in-house to perfection.

We believe coffee is more than a drink — it's a ritual, a moment of pause in a busy world.
